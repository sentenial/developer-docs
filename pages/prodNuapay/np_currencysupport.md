## Currency Support

Nuapay currently supports both Euro Payments and British Sterling payments.
<br/>Note that in the:

* SEPA payment schemes (CORE and B2B) all currencies are <b>EUR</b>
* BACS scheme the currency must be <b>GBP</b>.
